Lasagna recipe

Lasagna, a cherished Italian classic, consists of layers of pasta sheets and a flavorful, hearty sauce. The sauce is typically crafted using ground beef or pork, paired with a medley of onions, garlic, and tomatoes. To begin, you'll prepare the meat sauce by browning the meat in a large skillet.

Sauté finely chopped onions and garlic until they release their aromatic flavors and become translucent. Introduce crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and a blend of Italian herbs like basil, oregano, and thyme to create a savory sauce. While the sauce simmers to enhance its taste, turn your attention to the pasta sheets and the cheese mixture.

In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese, while adding beaten eggs, salt, and pepper for a rich and creamy mixture. Now, it's time to assemble your lasagna.

Start with a thin layer of the meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish, followed by a layer of pasta sheets, a spread of the cheese mixture, and a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella. Continue this layering process, concluding with a final layer of pasta sheets and a generous topping of mozzarella cheese.

Bake the lasagna until it reaches a bubbling, golden brown perfection around 30-40 minutes. Let it cool briefly before serving, and you'll relish a delightful, layered Italian masterpiece sure to satisfy your palate.

Ingredient:

  • Lasagna noodles (approximately 12-15 sheets)
  • 1 pound ground beef or ground pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 5 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large skillet, brown the ground beef or pork over medium heat.
    • Add finely chopped onions and minced garlic and sauté until they're softened and fragrant.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper.
    • Simmer the sauce for about 20-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  2. Cook the Lasagna Noodles:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente.
    • Drain and set the noodles aside.
  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzarella, grated Parmesan cheese, beaten eggs, salt, and pepper.
    • Mix the ingredients until well combined.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
    • Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Add a layer of the cheese mixture.
    • Sprinkle with some of the remaining shredded mozzarella.
    • Repeat these layers until you run out of ingredients, finishing with a layer of lasagna noodles.
  5. Bake the Lasagna:
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the top is golden and the lasagna is bubbling.
  6. Rest and Serve:
    • Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes before cutting and serving.
    • Optionally, garnish with fresh basil leaves or parsley for added flavor and presentation.
